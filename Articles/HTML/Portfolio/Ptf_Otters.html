OTTER'S HELL

Roguelite | Unity | C#

Published: 21/10/2024

As Director, Producer, and Lead Programmer of a team of 8, I developed "Otter's Hell", a roguelite shooter with procedurally generated environments.

  • Gameplay: Advanced AI enemies that patrol, chase the player, attack, and use special abilities. Challenging bosses with unique mechanics.
  • Technical aspects: Procedural level generation and dynamic combat systems programming.
  • Key Skills: Team leadership, AI development, procedural generation, performance optimization, and version control management using Plastic SC.
