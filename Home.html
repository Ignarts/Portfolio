WORK

Ignacio Meléndez is a professional developer with 2 years of experience in building applications using Unity and C#. During this time, he has been involved in several notable projects.

He served as the lead programmer, project manager, and producer for a Roguelite game, managing a team of 8 people. Additionally, he worked as a gameplay and UI/UX interfaces programmer for a multiplayer spaceship racing game called Racerloop , collaborating with a team of over 15 members. This project, available on the EPIC Games platform, also involved contributions to multiplayer systems.

He is well-versed in version control practices, utilizing tools such as Git, Git Extensions, GitHub, and Plastic. Known for his organization, discipline, and teamwork, he excels at code documentation, technical problem-solving, and game performance optimization.
